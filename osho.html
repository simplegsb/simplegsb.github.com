I was first introduced to Osho through the book "Tantra Experience". I was blown away by the way he wrote (or spoke as I later found out). He told the story so beautifully and was able to express spirituality without being really "spiritual". His meditations have easily been the most powerful meditative experiences of my life. No other meditations I have done have come close.

OSHO Information Center, Auckland

I created a basic website to provide information about Osho in New Zealand (primarily Osho in Auckland) and when I'm in town I try to help out where I can.

OSHO International Meditation Resort

I spent 3 months in the meditation resort, Pune, India (Nov 2014 - Feb 2015). It is a place of experimentation. There is a lot on offer, lots of OSHO meditations, the OSHO meditative therapies and various courses and sessions.

I did the Creative Living program and was lucky enough to attend 10 days of Tantra courses with Homa and Mukto.

Samaya Ashram

I spent 6 months at Samaya Ashram (the second half of 2013). It is a beautiful and intimate place filled with love and beautiful people. It is also a powerful place for self-transformation. Why did I leave again?

Oh... and this is where I got the name Prem Gyan. Prem = Love, Gyan = Wisdom. It was explained to me that I need to take the path of love to attain wisdom.

Free Speech Vs Free Beer

The projects we provide, we do so with complete freedom. You can grab one of our projects and, basically, do whatever you want with it. Richard Stallman, the founder of the Free Software Foundation and The GNU Project, explains that the 'free' in 'free software' does not elude to no cost (gratis) but to freedom. In his words:

“Free software” is a matter of liberty, not price. To understand the concept, you should think of “free” as in “free speech,” not as in “free beer.”

We've provided the freedom to you. If you like, you can provide the beer to us :)
